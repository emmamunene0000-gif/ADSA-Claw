KEY DESIGN DECISIONS:
  dollarrisk= not vol_dollar: EA calculates real lots from $ amount.
              Pine cannot know Deriv contract specs.
  entry=:     locked_entry (close at signal bar). Locked before strategy.entry().
  betrig=:    pips of profit to trigger BE move (from locked risk distance)
  bedist=:    pips beyond entry for BE price (from ts_be_buffer_pts input)
  trtrig=2:   hardcoded — trail activates at 2× ATR profit
  conf=:      locked AFTER claw_bull/bear_conf computed in Section 20
  py:         pyramid flag — EA allows multiple entries (pyramiding=10)

SECTION 5 — THE CLAW (Liquidity Trail)

Ratcheting ATR trail around 200 EMA:
  Bull: trail = max(trail[-1], ema - atr × mult)   only moves UP
  Bear: trail = min(trail[-1], ema + atr × mult)   only moves DOWN

Three MTF Claw trails:
  M5  claw — fastest, execution bias
  M15 claw — medium, session bias
  H1  claw — slowest, structural bias

Claw confidence contribution (highest weight 1.5x):
  All 3 bull → max confidence boost
  Mixed → partial boost
  All 3 bear → used for short direction

The Claw is NOT a trigger. It is the directional TRUTH.
ATM Bot fires WHEN. Claw defines WHICH WAY is allowed.

SECTION 6 — SIGNAL HOLDER MODE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Activates after TP3 hit (runner position remains open).

Kill switch: 34-period EMA of _bclose (signal smoothing line)
  Long holder: close crosses BELOW signal line → EXIT
  Short holder: close crosses ABOVE signal line → EXIT

Why signal line not VWAP:
  VWAP resets daily — intraday runner exits too early on daily reset.
  Signal smoothing is continuous — better for holding runners.

Holder trail options:
  "Signal"     → 34-period EMA kill switch (default, recommended)
  "Structural" → holder_trail_price (ratcheting structural trail)

SECTION 7 — PERFORMANCE TRACKING
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

DPT Arrays (per trade):
  dpt_dir        1=long, -1=short
  dpt_entry_px   entry price at signal bar
  dpt_exit_px    actual exit price (set at close event)
  dpt_net_pips   signed actual P&L in pips
  dpt_tp1_hit    TP1 partial close achieved
  dpt_sl_hit     SL or orphan exit

ORPHAN TRADE RESOLUTION:
  When new opposite signal fires while trade is running:
  → Close old trade at current bar's close
  → Compute signed net pips
  → Flag as sl_hit if TP1 not yet hit
  → Reset dpt_active_idx = -1
  → New trade opens fresh

Net pips formula:
  math.round(_pips(direction × (exit_price − entry_price)), 1)
  Positive = profitable exit, Negative = loss

Daily Report (21:00 UTC):
  Per-trade log with: direction, entry time, score, outcome, net pips, peak pips, bars held
  Session breakdown: LDN / NY counts
  Performance: WR%, PF, net pts
